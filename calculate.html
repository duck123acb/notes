Calculation Help


  • Hit: "Alt + c" on your keyboard with the math statement highlighted
  • Formatting: "number-one operator number-two" || E.G.: "10 + 5"
  • Valid operators:
  • + | addition
  • - | subtraction
  • * | multiplication
  • / | division
  • ^ | exponents (ex: to the power of)
  • /^ | roots (ex: square root)
